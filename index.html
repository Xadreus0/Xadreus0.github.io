About Me

From an early age, I found a strong sense of satisfaction in solving problems and understanding how things work, which naturally grew into a career in engineering. I am completing my final year of Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering, with a strong interest in manufacturing automation, embedded systems, and interface-level programming across C, C++, and VHDL.

During my internship at Price Holyoake, I applied these interests to real-world manufacturing challenges by designing and building two low-budget, semi-automated laser-welding machines. These systems are now fully integrated into the production line and used for every LFDC unit, eliminating reliance on specialised welding labour while improving consistency and throughput.

Now working full-time as an Automation Technician, I support and enhance production machinery, develop custom products, optimise manufacturing processes, and assist in the design of new semi-automated production lines, including a major upcoming diffuser line to strengthen sovereign manufacturing.

My experience across welding, PLC systems, CNC design, electronics, PCB design and development, and factory maintenance has shaped me into a highly curious, hands-on engineer who thrives in multidisciplinary environments. I am motivated by the unique challenges each project presents and aim to continue developing practical, production-focused automation solutions as I progress toward an engineering career.
